Screen 6
Ledger — the retention product
Everything reads from user_captures: hero total via getLedgerSummary(), monthly bars and category breakdown from the same rows grouped by month/category, recent list ordered by captured_at. The pace projection annualizes YTD — and deliberately echoes the $340/yr market stat. "Share my recap" renders a shareable image (no personal data beyond the totals) — the product's only viral surface, so it lives here. Renewal pitch and Pro paywall arguments both point at this screen.
